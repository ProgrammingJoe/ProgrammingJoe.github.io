about

My interests revolve around solving real world problems with practical solutions. Most of my experience is with developing web platforms including designing databases, improving development tools, building API's, developing front-end features, fine tuning visuals, hosting, and everything in between. However, I'm also interested in expanding my knowledge into more advanced artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing techniques.

I also enjoy tackling the challenges that both the business and team management worlds have to offer. My number one goal with software projects is to make a meaningful difference in the world and I believe that a sound business plan is required to make that happen. Developing these business plans myself provides another level of understanding and challenge to my work. Working with and managing teams is also an interest of mine. I'd love to work on creating work environments where people are working at their best, enjoying what they do, and supporting each other in their shared venture.

In my spare time I enjoy working on side projects, playing video games, rock climbing, and a variety of other activities.
